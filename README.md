## Overview

Three interactive visualizations accompanying [The New Proliferation Era](https://northbranchmedia.substack.com/p/1ec750f4-a227-4c93-b4e8-d08f32555e52) published by North Branch Media. The piece argues that nuclear proliferation is likely to accelerate as the US extended deterrence umbrella faces mounting credibility questions, and examines nine threshold states positioned to pursue breakout.

### Global Nuclear Capabilities Map

Interactive choropleth displaying the current nuclear landscape. Countries are filled by nuclear status (self-sponsored, US umbrella, Russia umbrella, NPT signatory, non-NPT). Markers indicate nuclear storage sites, SSBN patrol zones and active weapons programs. Sub-12-month breakout states are crosshatched. Built with D3.js and TopoJSON on an Equal Earth projection.

### Warhead Counts Treemap

Area-proportional treemap of global nuclear warhead inventories across all nine nuclear-armed states. Each cell is filled with the country's flag and labeled with total warhead count. Built with D3.js treemap layout.

### Breakout Scorecards

Nine-country breakout assessment organized in three regional rows: Middle East (Iran, Saudi Arabia, Egypt), East Asia (South Korea, Japan, Taiwan) and Europe (Poland, Germany, Turkey). Each card includes nuclear sponsor, enrichment capability, fissile stock, nuclear electricity share, missile range, nuclear partners and proliferation driver. Built with D3.js.
